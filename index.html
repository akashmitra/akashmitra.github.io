About Akash

Well...since Akash Mitra himself has designed and developed this website...lets not address myself in third person. About me's are generally towards the end. But then initially I made a weird wireframing (happens when you try to nick templates from bootstrap) and later got way too lazy to move it down below the gallery. However, lets get to the point.

I am Akash Mitra, an IT Analyst by profession and a Photoenthusiast by enthusiasm (duh!). I mostly work on Street & Social Documentary, and sometimes Fine Art Photography.

Apart from this, I love Astronomy & Stargazing, Reading & Writing (..Poems...if you ask!), Music (ofcourse), eating..cooking..sleeping..drinking...and a whole lot of things.
